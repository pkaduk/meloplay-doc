Practice — Build Real Skills

Notes Trainer

Read notes on the treble staff. In song mode, follow a scrolling staff and tap each note in time — from Ode to Joy to Swan Lake. In practice mode, work through random notes matched to your level.

Fretboard Trainer

Find note positions across the neck. Beginner mode uses letter names for natural notes. Advanced mode adds staff reading and the full chromatic scale.

Ear Trainer

Identify single notes (with a reference C), intervals (melodic and harmonic), and chords. Pick a category, choose your level, and build a streak.

Learn Theory — Chords, Scales & Relationships

Chord Builder

Stack notes into chords on interactive theory blocks. The app shows what's missing and explains intervals — so you understand why a chord sounds the way it does.

Chord Browser

Pick a root and chord type, see fretboard diagrams, swipe between voicings, hear strum or arpeggio playback, and save favorites.

Scale Visualizer

See how scales map onto the staff and fretboard at once. Choose key and scale type — major, minor, pentatonic, and church modes.

Scale Builder

Build scales note by note on theory blocks or a mini fretboard. Hear the finished scale ascending and descending.
